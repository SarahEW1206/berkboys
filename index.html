Welcome to Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream!

Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream grew out of our 6-year old CEO Ryan Berkowitz’s desire to earn enough money to buy a Nintendo Switch. Ryan’s mom, Jen, an experienced CEO herself, thought about her newfound hobby of making delicious ice cream at home and realized it could be the perfect thing for Ryan to sell. So we perfected our recipes and began selling pints to our neighbors.

Berk Boys has really taken off, and we now have many satisfied, repeat customers. We’re also available in Seven Bridges’ 7Prime clubhouse cafe! Thank you for your continuing support of Berk Boys.

Thanks to you, Ryan has surpassed his initial goal, and now he’s interested in making his business grow and grow!
