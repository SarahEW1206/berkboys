Welcome to Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream!

Hi! I'm Ryan Berkowitz. I'm 6, and I'm the CEO of Berk Boys. Originally, I just wanted to earn enough money to buy myself a Nintendo Switch. It was taking a long time to save up my allowance, so I decided to sell something. At first I wasn't sure what to sell, but then my mom had a great idea - ice cream! My mom and I have been making ice cream together since I was 3 years old. We come up with fun, tasty flavors and everyone who comes to our house always loves it. I knew it would be the perfect thing to sell!

At first I thought that ALL the money we made would go toward my Switch, but then my mom taught me about income and expenses, and how that contributes to profit and loss. She knows a lot about business, so even though some of our money has to go to our expenses, it's worth it because I get to learn a lot from Mom about how it all works!

Anyway, ever since my mom announced our "virtual ice cream stand" on Facebook, we've been selling a ton! I'm having so much fun and I made enough money for my Switch in the first week! We started with four flavors and now we have eight. And now our ice cream is being served at Prime 7 restaurant at 7 Bridges clubhouse. It's already the biggest hit in the dessert section.

It's a lot of work having a business, but I love it. Sometimes, when we're really busy, I let my brother Jake help. He's the little guy on the label. We're all really proud of our accomplishment so far and can't wait to keep on growing. Thanks for visiting, and enjoy your Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream! It's made with LOVE!


Sincerely,
