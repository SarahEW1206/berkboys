Welcome to Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream!

Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream grew out of our 6-year old CEO Ryan Berkowitz’s desire to learn more about business and how profit and loss works when you make and sell a product. Ryan simply wanted to learn a little something in the process of making enough money to buy his own X-Box. Ryan’s mom, Jen, an experienced CEO herself, thought about her newfound hobby of making delicious ice cream at home and realized it could be the perfect product for Ryan to sell. So they perfected their recipes, designed some labels and did a little marketing online and in their Delray Beach, Florida neighborhood. Berk Boys has really taken off, and we now have many satisfied, repeat customers. We’re also available in Seven Bridges’ 7Prime clubhouse cafe! Thank you for your continuing support of Berk Boys. Thanks to you, Ryan has surpassed his initial goal of making enough money to buy his own X-Box, and now he’s interested in making his business grow and grow!
