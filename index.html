Welcome to Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream!

Hi! I'm Ryan and I'm 6 years old. I'm the CEO of Berk Boys. Cool, right? Originally, I just wanted to sell a few pints of homemade ice cream in my neighborhood to earn enough money to buy a Nintendo Switch. My mom and I have been making ice cream together since I was 3, and everyone always loves it, so we decided it was the perfect thing to sell.

Well, it turns out my neighbors really like ice cream! Ever since my mom announced our "virtual ice cream stand" on Facebook, we've been selling a ton! I'm having so much fun and I'm learning a lot about things like income and expenses. It's a lot of work to have a business, but I love it. Sometimes, if we're really busy, I let my brother Jake help. He's the little guy on the label.

We're very proud of what we've accomplished so far and we're excited for what's next. Thanks so much for visiting, and enjoy your Berk Boys Homemade Ice Cream! It's made with LOVE!


Sincerely,
